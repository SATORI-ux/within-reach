Within Reach

SMS opt-in details

This page documents the opt-in flow for urgent SMS alerts used by Within Reach. Messages are non-marketing and limited to urgent “Can you talk?” notifications.

Use case

Within Reach is a private, invite-only check-in experience. SMS is only used for urgent alerts when one participant taps “Can you talk?” and needs the other person’s attention quickly.

How consent is collected

  • The intended recipient explicitly agrees to receive urgent SMS alerts.
  • Consent is direct, one-to-one, and limited to non-marketing account notifications.
  • The sender stores the recipient’s phone number only after that agreement is given.
  • Message frequency is low and event-driven only.

What messages are sent

Within Reach: Joey needs you. Open Within Reach when you can. Reply STOP to opt out.

Opt-out and help

  • Recipients can opt out at any time by replying STOP.
  • Recipients can request help by replying HELP.
  • The sender can also disable SMS alerts manually at any time.

Message type

These messages are not promotional or marketing-related. They are personal account notifications for urgent contact only.
